About the Project

The Richmind AI Blockchain (Rich_AI_Chain) is an innovative blockchain ecosystem project by Richmind AI Limited, “The Bridge For Economic Empowerment”, to revolutionize the digital landscape and decentralized finance (DeFi) by integrating artificial intelligence (AI) with blockchain technology. Baby AI Coin (BAIC) is an Exchange Traded Funds (ETF) created as backbone to power our AI Blockchain project.
